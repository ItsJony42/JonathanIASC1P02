Information of the Hashira

Top Three
The Hashira is a rank among the Demon Slayer Corps.
They are strong swordsman that weild impeccable skills and have
experience in killing demons. There nine members of the Hashira,
and each one has their own unique breathing style.
Gyomei Himejima is a stone breather, and he is the
strongest of the Hashira. Sanemi Shinazugawa is
a wind breather, and he is definitely the second strongest. Sanemi
is also my second favourite Hashira. Giyu Tomioka
is the third best and he is also the main Hashira that the story
focuses on.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Coolest Cursed Techniques

  • Idle Transfiguration
  • Limitless
  • Cursed Speech

Jujutsu Kaisen has some of the coolest powers
in fiction, and my top three favourite ones are
Idle Transfiguration, Limitless, and Cursed Speech.
Idle Transfiguration is a unique and cool
technique that allows the owner of the technique
to physically maniulate the target's soul, which
shapes their body into whatever they want it to
be shaped as. Limitless is another one of my
favourite techniques. It is used to repel anything
around the user, making them untouchable. Finally,
my absolute favourite cursed technique is Cursed
Speech. It allows the user to use their words and
compel others to do what they say. This is an
incredibly cool technique, and it can be used in
various ways to defeat enemies and win fights.
