Infinity Saga

The second saga of the MCU is the Multiverse Saga, and this saga explores the multiverse and the different timelines of the MCU. This saga is what brought all the other Marvel franchises together - Daredevil, the X-Men, even the old Blade movies. It's currently still going, and it'll eventually lead to another two-part climax, similar to Infinity War and Endgame.
